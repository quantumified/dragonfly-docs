How to Update Sector Data

This section provides instructions on how to update sector data for the bot.

  1. Step 1: Go to FOE.
  2. Step 2: Open GBG.
  3. Step 3: Press the GBG icon in FOEHelper.
  4. Step 4: Press "Select All".
  5. Step 5: Press "Copy".
  6. Step 6: Type /updatesector on Discord.
  7. Step 7: Paste data in the data field.
  8. Step 8: Select your timezone in the timezone field.
