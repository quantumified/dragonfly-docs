How to Update Sector Data

This section provides instructions on how to update sector data for the bot.

  1. Open to FoE.
  2. Open GBG.
  3. Press the GBG icon in FoE-Helper.
  4. Press "Select All".
  5. Press "Copy".
  6. Type /updatedata on Discord.
  7. Paste data in the data field.
  8. Select your timezone in the timezone field.
